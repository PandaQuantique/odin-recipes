"Baked Ziti"

"Description"

"Baked ziti is an Italian meat, cheese and noodle dish. This recipe is easy to make and feel free to modify to taste or preference!"

"Ingredients"

  • "1 pound ziti"
  • "1.5 tablespoons olive oil"
  • "1 onion"
  • "1 teaspoon rosemary"
  • "4 garlic cloves"
  • "1 pound ground beef"
  • "1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • "6 ounces provolone"
  • "6 ounces mozzarella"
