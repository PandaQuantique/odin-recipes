Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is an Indian curry with wide appeal. This recipe makes a mild-medium version. It goes well with naan and mango chutney!

Ingredients

  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 4 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 piece of ginger, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons masala curry paste
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cubed
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cilantro, chopped

Steps

  1. Combine tomatoes, yogurt, garlic, and ginger in blender until smooth
  2. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Add onion and fry 3-4 minutes, stirring constantly. Add paste and fry until fragrant (about 1 minute), stirring occasionally. Add tomate mixture and chicken to pan and mix together. Season with salt and pepper. Remove pan from heat.
  3. Mix water and flour together in a bowl. Stir into chicken mixture in frying pan. Return pan to heat and bring to a boil, stirring constantly for 5 minutes. Reduce heat to low, cover and cook until thickened (about 15 minutes). Sprinkle with cilantro and serve.
