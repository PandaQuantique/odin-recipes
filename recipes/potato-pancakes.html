"Potato Pancakes"

"Description"

"Potato pancakes are a German dish. They can be used as either an entree or a side dish, and are frequently served with sour cream and applesauce."

"Ingredients"

  • "2 large eggs"
  • "2 tablespoons all-purpose flour"
  • "1/4 teaspoon baking powder"
  • "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  • "1/4 teaspoon pepper"
  • "6 medium potatoes, peeled and shredded"
  • "1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • "1/4 cup vegetable oil"

"Steps"

  • "Beat eggs, flour, baking powder, salt, and pepper together in large bowl."
  • "Add potatoes and onion to mixutre"
  • "Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add large tablespoonful of mix to pan, press to flatten. Cook until brown and crisp, approx. 3 minutes per side. Transfer to paper towel covered plate."
  • "Repeat until mixture is gone.
  • "Serve with preferred garnish, e.g. sour cream and applesauce."
